Start manual. Prove trust. Automate later.

Build the service around real requests, not app features nobody uses.

Launch with trusted runners, referral customers, clear pricing, and manual support before building a full app.

Launch strategy

  1. Start with one or two trusted runners.
  2. Take limited customers by referral or partner introduction.
  3. Run requests manually through a simple form and text updates.
  4. Track time, miles, wait time, parking, customer issues, and runner satisfaction.
  5. Build the app after patterns are proven.

Best first partners

Dry cleaners, pharmacies, travel agencies, immigration attorneys, high-rise buildings, law offices, accountants, real estate offices, medical offices, and senior communities.

Revenue streams

Route fees

First stop, additional stops, long-distance route quotes.

Priority fees

Higher pricing for faster windows and scarce runner availability.

Mission-critical fees

Airport rescue, passport retrieval, medication, legal documents, urgent business materials.

Administrative services

Passport office, DMV, embassy, permits, paperwork drop-offs and pickups.

Business accounts

Recurring work from offices and buildings that need reliable runners.

Live support

Optional human help for customers who do not want to rely only on a form.

Positioning: not the cheapest, not the fastest in every case. The most transparent and reliable local helper when the job matters.
