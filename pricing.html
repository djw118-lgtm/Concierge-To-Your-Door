Pricing Framework

This pricing model is designed to start simple, stay transparent, and avoid asking a brand-new customer for a full membership fee before they experience the service.

Beta Usage Fee

$5 per completed request

This works like a gradual membership contribution. Instead of charging $50 upfront, the customer pays as they use the service.

Founding Member Cap

Up to $50/month

Once the customer reaches the monthly cap, the beta usage fee can stop for the month. Route, rush, parking, tolls, and special services still apply.

Priority and emergency services

Standard

Same day or scheduled. Best price. No emergency promise.

Priority

Target within 2-4 hours. Adds a priority fee when capacity exists.

Rush

Target within 60-90 minutes. Higher fee. Runner must accept knowing route and time.

Mission Critical

Passport, medication, legal documents, laptop, airport rescue. Custom quote based on time, traffic, runner location, and risk.

Example: Airport Passport Rescue

Customer is at O'Hare. Passport is downtown. Runner is 10 minutes from pickup. Drive to airport may take 45-60 minutes.

  • Emergency / mission fee: $50+
  • Route and time fee: $40-$75+
  • Parking/tolls if required: receipt reimbursement
  • Estimated total: $90-$150+, depending on actual urgency and runner acceptance
