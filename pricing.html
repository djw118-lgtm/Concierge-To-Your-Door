Weight and handling tiers

Light

0-25 lbs: included for simple jobs when no extra effort is required.

Medium

26-99 lbs: add $10-$20 depending on stairs, distance to vehicle, and item count.

Heavy

100-249 lbs: add $25-$50 and confirm vehicle fit before dispatch.

Bulk

250-499 lbs: custom quote, dolly recommended, runner must approve details.

Commercial load

500+ lbs or pallet-style work: van/truck quote only, no surprise acceptance.

Special handling

Fragile, medical, legal, luxury, or signature-required items can trigger extra proof and pricing.
