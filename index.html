What it is

Concierge Next Door is not trying to be another food-delivery app. It is a limited-access local assistance network for pickups, returns, paperwork, emergency item retrieval, retail orders, dry cleaning, permitted pharmacy pickups, and business errands.

Core promise: clear price, clear runner pay, clear proof, no hidden formula.

Best service categories

Retail Pickup

Walmart, Target, Best Buy, Home Depot, Bloomingdale's, Macy's, Costco, Sam's Club.

Package & Returns

UPS, FedEx, USPS, DHL, Amazon returns, business packages, prepaid labels.

Personal Rescue

Passport, laptop, keys, wallet, badge, charger, medicine, travel documents.

Administrative Concierge

Passport office, DMV, embassy paperwork, permits, clerk offices, document pickup/drop-off.

Business Errands

Law offices, real estate offices, medical offices, contracts, supplies, office materials.

Catering Only

Food delivery is not the focus. Catering can be accepted only when the price, setup, distance, and proof requirements make sense.

The service culture

Respect the customer

Clear expectations before the job starts.

Respect the runner

Guaranteed pay, visible time, visible miles, and no punishment culture for fair declines.

Protect the handoff

Hand delivery first, photo/GPS/signature proof when the job requires it.
