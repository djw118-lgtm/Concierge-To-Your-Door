What it is

Concierge Next Door is not trying to be another DoorDash or Uber Eats. It is a limited-access personal assistance network for pickups, returns, paperwork, emergency item retrieval, retail orders, dry cleaning, pharmacy pickups where permitted, and business errands.

The customer pays the merchant directly whenever possible. The service handles the mission: pickup, verification, transport, handoff, and proof.

Core promise

Clear price. Clear runner pay. Clear proof. No hidden formula.
