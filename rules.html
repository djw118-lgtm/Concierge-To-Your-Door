Operating Rules

Hand Delivery First

Default delivery is hand-to-customer or hand-to-authorized person. Porch drop-off is only allowed when the customer explicitly authorizes it.

Escalation

If the runner cannot contact the customer: text, call, wait, contact support, follow saved fallback instructions, then return or hold item if needed.

Proof of completion

Delivery typeRequired proof
Standard hand deliveryGPS timestamp, handoff note, optional photo if appropriate.
Doorman/front deskName or desk note, lobby/front desk photo if allowed, GPS timestamp.
Porch drop-offItem photo, door photo, curb/house photo when safe, GPS timestamp.
High-value orderSignature or verified recipient, pickup photo, handoff proof, optional short video.
Government/document jobReceipt, ticket, photo proof where allowed, parking receipts, timestamped updates.

High-value and sensitive items

Items like passports, luxury retail, electronics, legal documents, medicine, and high-value merchandise require extra proof. The system should classify these before the runner accepts.

Verified

Photo, GPS, customer confirmation.

Signature Required

Customer or authorized recipient must sign or confirm receipt.

Video Proof

Optional for high-value jobs. Short clip only when appropriate and privacy-safe.

Customer conduct: customers who repeatedly mistreat runners, file false claims, give abusive instructions, abuse emergency status, or use insulting tips may lose access.
Good runners and good customers are both protected.
